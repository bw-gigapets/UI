About Us

At GigaPet, we pride ourselves on bringing health education to families in a safe, fun and organic way, having children and parents come together as partners in health to fight this global epidemic. The future may be in their hands, but their nutrition is in ours. We hope you enjoy our small contribution to forming healthy habits with a smiles and laughs.

This website and app were built in collaboration with backend help from Lambda School students and staff. We are a group of Lambda School students showcasing our technical skills by developing a real-life web-app experience. This project is meant to be an alpha prototype, so there may be incomplete parts on purpose. Please let us know if there are any issues or errors, so we can make it better!
